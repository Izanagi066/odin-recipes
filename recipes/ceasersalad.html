ceaser salad

Description

A healthy and cheap option for those who are trying to lose weight but still have a tasty meal.

Needed Ingredients

  • romaine lettuce
  • croutons
  • parmesan cheese
  • a rich dressing

Steps

  1. prepare the croutons by cutting small cubes of white bread and baking them for 20 minutes with olive oil for taste
  2. grate or shave the parmesan cheese
  3. prepare the dressing by mixing olive oil,garlic,egg yolk and salt
  4. put the lettuce into a bowl and mix everything
