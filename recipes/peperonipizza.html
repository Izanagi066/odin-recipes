Steps

  1. To prepare the dough: in the Masterchef Gourmet stainless steel bowl, combine the flour with the sugar, dried yeast and salt.
  2. Add the water and 2 tablespoons of olive oil then knead the ingredients well using the dough hook until the dough forms into a ball. If the dough is too sticky, add 1 or 2 teaspoons of flour, and if its too dry add a little bit of water.
  3. Drizzle the inside of a big bowl with 2 teaspoons of olive oil and put the dough inside the bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and let rest in a warm place for about an hour until doubled in volume.
  4. Flatten the dough ball on a floured surface into a large circle using a rolling pin.
  5. Transfer the dough on the baking sheet and leave to rest for 10 minutes.
